# DigiiMark — Services Pricing Guide

## Engagement Models

DigiiMark offers three engagement models:
1. **One-Time Projects** — Fixed scope, fixed price. Ideal for website builds, audits, automation setups.
2. **Monthly Retainers** — Ongoing strategy + execution. Ideal for SEO, content, and growth.
3. **Performance-Based** — Revenue-share model available for select clients.
